What are petitions?

To show support towards a certain cause, in this case the Black Lives Movement (BLM), one can sign petitions. Petitions are means to appeal to authorities. By having many signatatures, a petition raises awareness and showcases to the authorities that many approve of its aim. Thus, signing petitions is one way to show support towards the BLM without risking one's health to COVID-19.

Can one still sign petitions even if they don't live in the U.S.?

Yes! Even if one does not live in the U.S., they can still use U.S. zip codes to sign petitions. For example, they can use 90015 (Los Angeles, California), 10001 (New York City, New York), and 75001 (Dallas, Texas).
